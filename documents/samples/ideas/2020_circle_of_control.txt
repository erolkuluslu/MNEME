TYPE: Mental Model
SOURCE: Stoic Philosophy (Epictetus)
TITLE: The Circle of Control
AUTHOR: Me
DATE: 2020-09-20

The framework:

Draw two circles.

INNER CIRCLE (Your control):
- Your thoughts
- Your effort
- Your reactions
- Your character
- How you treat others

OUTER CIRCLE (Not your control):
- Other people's actions
- The economy
- Politics
- Weather
- The past
- Most of the future
- What others think of you

The practice:

When anxiety hits, ask: "Is this in my inner circle?"

If yes: Act. Do something about it.
If no: Accept. Stop wasting energy.

Why this helps me:

90% of my anxiety is about things outside my control. Recognizing this doesn't make worry disappear, but it loosens its grip.
