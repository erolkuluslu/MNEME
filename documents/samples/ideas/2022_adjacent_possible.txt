TYPE: Mental Model
SOURCE: Steven Johnson / Where Good Ideas Come From
TITLE: The Adjacent Possible
AUTHOR: Me
DATE: 2022-06-10

The concept:

Innovation happens in the "adjacent possible"â€”what's one step from current reality.

You can't jump three steps. But you can step into the adjacent space, which opens new adjacent spaces.

Why it matters:

Ideas "ahead of their time" fail. They're too far from the adjacent possible.

YouTube couldn't exist before broadband. The iPhone couldn't exist before touchscreens, batteries, and wireless networks.

Application:

DON'T: Try to build the final vision immediately.

DO: Build what's possible now. It opens new possibilities.

Career version:

You can't become anything from where you are. But you can become what's adjacent.

Each step expands what's adjacent.

From where I am, I can't become a brain surgeon. But I can become a machine learning engineer, which might lead to medical AI, which is adjacent to healthcare...

The path:

Step into adjacent possibles. Over time, you end up somewhere you couldn't have reached directly.
