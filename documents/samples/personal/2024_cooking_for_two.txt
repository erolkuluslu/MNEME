TITLE: Learning to Cook for Two
AUTHOR: Me
DATE: 2024-04-20

Living with Maya means cooking for two. It's different than cooking alone.

Challenges:
- Recipe scaling (portions often off)
- Accounting for preferences (she's vegetarian on weekdays)
- Coordinating schedules (who cooks when?)
- Grocery shopping together (takes forever but it's bonding time)

Our system:
- Sunday: Meal prep day. Big batch of grains, roasted veggies, prepped proteins.
- Monday-Thursday: She cooks Mon/Wed, I cook Tue/Thu. Simple meals.
- Friday: Date night out or fancy cook-together.
- Weekend: Freestyle. Brunch experiments. Sourdough projects.

I've gotten better at vegetarian cooking. Roasted cauliflower with tahini. Mushroom bolognese. Black bean tacos.
