The problem:

Traditional sources of meaning have declined:
- Religion
- Community
- Stable careers
- Clear life scripts

What's left? Consumer choice. Not enough.

Symptoms:

- Rising anxiety, depression
- "Deaths of despair"
- Nihilism, especially in young people
- Searching in ideologies, cults, online communities

John Vervaeke's framework:

Three crises intertwined:
1. Crisis of meaning (what matters?)
2. Crisis of wisdom (how to live?)
3. Crisis of community (who do I belong to?)

Potential answers:

- Practices (meditation, contemplation)
- Communities of practice
- Re-connection to embodiment
- Rediscovering wisdom traditions
